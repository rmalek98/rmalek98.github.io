About

Welcome to My World, a platform created by food lover and culinary enthusiast, Zahra Zynab.


Our mission is to provide a space where individuals who share a passion for cooking and baking can come together to share knowledge, experiences, and inspiration. We aim to foster a community that encourages exploration and creativity in the kitchen.


This Site was born out of Zahra's love for food and her desire to connect with others who share her passion. As a student pursuing a double major in Hospitality Management and Food and Beverages at ECU, Zahra saw the potential for a platform that would bring food enthusiasts together. She began working on the website in 2023 with the help of Malek, thriving for a community of food lovers.


When she's not studying or working on the website, Zahra enjoys watching cooking shows, trying out new recipes, practicing yoga, working out, and taking care of her skin.
