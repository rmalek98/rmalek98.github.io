Pasta with Pesto and Tomatoes

This quick and delicious cheese pizza is the perfect way to indulge in a classic comfort food. With gooey melted cheese and a crispy crust, it's sure to satisfy any pizza cravings.

Don't have time to make pizza dough from scratch? No problem! You can easily make this pizza using a pre-made crust or even a frozen pizza crust.

  • Servings: 2
  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10-12 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes

The Ingredients

  • Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  • Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface until it's about 12 inches in diameter.
  • Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or a sheet of parchment paper.
  • Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving about 1/2 inch around the edges.
  • Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  • Sprinkle the grated parmesan cheese over the mozzarella.
  • Drizzle the olive oil over the cheese.
  • Add salt and pepper to taste.
  • Transfer the pizza to the preheated pizza stone or place the parchment paper on a baking sheet and place in the oven.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the crust is golden brown.
  • Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Tips

    • For an extra crispy crust, preheat your pizza stone in the oven for at least 30 minutes before baking your pizza on it.
    • You can also sprinkle some garlic powder or dried oregano over the pizza before baking for added flavor.
    • If you like a little spice, try adding some red pepper flakes to the sauce or sprinkling them over the pizza before baking.

There you have it, a classic cheese pizza that you can make at home. Experiment with different cheeses or toppings to make it your own. Enjoy!
